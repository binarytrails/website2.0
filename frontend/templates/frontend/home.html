Short Movies

Back in High School, I started searching different ways to express myself through animated arts. I read a lot about Adobe After Effect and I spent a lifetime mastering it. I loved taking time after school trying to create something new.

During the last High School year, I made my first short film with a friend of mine. I managed to create awesome special effects. It was such a great experience! It gave me drive to create video montages freely for the community to encourage its growth.

I always love to take some of my time to sit down after work on an inspiration warm evening. I try to let my feelings guide me in the creation of new short movies.

Photography

It started at the same time that my passion for animated arts was making its roots. I wasn't very good with Adobe Photoshop and had mastered Adobe After Effect.

People always get surprised when learning about my use of AE as an image editing software. An advantage of its use is to be able to turn the image editing project to an animated project in a light strike.

After I moved to Linux, I started searching for open source alternatives. I found GIMP and I loved it. It lacks a few features but it has a hudge potential in achieving as much as the Adobe suite.

I am using my abilities on a daily life basis to integrate artistic designing into my projects.
