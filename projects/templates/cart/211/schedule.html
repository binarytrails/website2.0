Assignment_05: Social & Sharing Economy :
In teams of 2-3 create a website that proposes a social or sharing economy (see readings). This website should offer a useful solution to a local or known problem that people can access or participate in online as a community. The site can be in French or English.

Consider specific sites (urban context, work, home, hospital etc.) that you are familiar with, which addresses a current social problematic. This project builds on the previous project in which we explored: data visualization; internet of things.

You will need to closely consider many aspects of your design proposal, development and testing. Your design should be beneficial to the society we live in today (quality of life) and take into consideration both the seemingly limitless possibilities of networked information as well as its limitations (social, technical, cultural).It should also keep in mind the various communities that make up our day-to-day: youth, elderly, disabled, economic etc.
Write a short (200 word) summary of the concept and technical elements of the piece along with how your idea expands on this.

Due in 2 parts (Nov. 10 and 17th)
1. 200 word proposal/summary
2. Wireframe of the website interface, this will be what is required at a mimimum. From this base try adding as much extra functionality or interactivity as you can.
3. Research the audience viability. ie. Is there an audience or interested user base for your idea?
