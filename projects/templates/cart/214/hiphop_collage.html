Hip-Hop Culture Collage

Create a digital collage using the terms "Rhythm, and Balance". In this work you will create a an image out of "found" imagery, either from the net, magazines or elsewhere.

I made this piece as a reminder of time and how it changed Hip-Hop culture. This culture came from Funk with legendary James Brown (as seen on left side) and evolved over time by expanding all over continents. It is made of four elements: Graffiti, MC'ing, Bboying & DJ'ing. This Bboy (on the right) practicing the art of Bboying is Intact from Ruffneck Attack (Ukraine). He is the "God Father" of this type of dance in his country. In the background, we can see the 5 Pointz murral of New York city, a patrimoine of Hip-Hop culture that was destroyed overnight in 2014, ignoring all of the petitions.

Everything is constantly mutating, nothing is permanent. I think that this piece gathers together different stages of Hip-Hop culture to capture them in time.
